SMART DETECTOR

Real Time Disease and Pest Detector

Smart Detect is a flexible and easy use application for aiding in the identification of rice diseases and pests . The aim is to help farmers to identify problems in the growing crop and Prevent loss of rice yield. !

Features

Smart Detect is mobile application that help farmer to detect rice disease and pest in real-time without any internet connection and prevents huge loss by taking precautions earlier.

Portable

Use Smart Detect wherever you are. No data connection required once data sources are downloaded .

Android Support

Smart Detect supports Android.

Optimized Layout

The app is optimized to work on both phones and tablets and varying screen sizes.

Material Design

Smart Detect follows Google's Material Design Guidelines.

Import Image

Upload infected plant Image and Smart Detect Will Anilize and gives you the result
